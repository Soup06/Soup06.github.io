Terms of submission

Publicity

All information submitted by you is stored in our database, and is public for anyone to see. It is strongly advised against submitting personal information.
It is also important to note that the information you submit is permanent, and none of this information will be removed from our site or our database.


Guidelines

The Archive of Aleatoria is intended to be accessible to people age 11 and up. Therefore, any content, text, or images that you post must be safe, respectful, and appropriate for any people of this age.


Responsibility of truth

We take no responsibility over any of the information on our site, or its accuracy. False information, intentional or unintentional, is not our responsibility, and we will take no blame for its outcome. It is your responsibility to decide what to believe.


Statistics and fairness

Automated processes, such as the calculation of average review ratings or the random displaying of scenes, are fair and unmodified. None of these processes have been altered in favour of any particular person.
